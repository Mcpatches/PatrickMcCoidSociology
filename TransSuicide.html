Transgender Suicide and Support Resources

Understanding the risks and the resources available to support transgender individuals, including The Trevor Project.

Suicide Risk

Studies show that transgender individuals experience suicide attempts at significantly higher rates than the general population, with youth at particular risk due to bullying, discrimination, and social stigma.

Mental Health Challenges

Transgender individuals often face high rates of depression, anxiety, and trauma. Lack of acceptance, discrimination, and limited access to healthcare exacerbate these challenges.

How The Trevor Project Can Help

The Trevor Project provides crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to LGBTQ+ youth, including trained counselors available via phone, text, and chat. Their resources can be lifesaving for transgender youth struggling with suicidal thoughts.
