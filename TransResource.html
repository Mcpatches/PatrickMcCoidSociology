Supporting the Transgender Community

These organizations provide critical resources, advocacy, and support for transgender and gender-diverse individuals across the United States.

The Trevor Project

Visit Website »

The Trevor Project focuses on suicide prevention and crisis intervention for LGBTQ+ youth. Their trained counselors are available 24/7, offering immediate help to transgender individuals facing mental health challenges. By providing confidential support, education, and resources, The Trevor Project saves lives and empowers young people to thrive.

Chapin Hall

Visit Website »

Chapin Hall conducts research on youth well-being and social policies. Their studies on homelessness and health inequities among transgender youth provide vital data for governments and organizations. By highlighting disparities, Chapin Hall enables targeted programs to protect and uplift transgender communities across the nation.

My Life Is Worth Living

Visit Website »

This organization is committed to suicide prevention, particularly for LGBTQ+ youth. Through digital campaigns, peer-to-peer support, and education, they provide tools for coping, resilience, and connection. Their work empowers transgender individuals to understand that their life is valuable and they are not alone.

Impact Fund – Discrimination Cases

Visit Website »

Impact Fund provides legal support and advocacy for communities facing discrimination. By litigating cases related to transgender rights, they help protect civil liberties, challenge harmful policies, and ensure equal treatment under the law.

National Center for Transgender Equality

Visit Website »

The NCTE advocates for policy change at the federal, state, and local level to protect transgender individuals. They focus on areas such as healthcare access, employment protections, and anti-discrimination laws. Their advocacy strengthens societal structures so that transgender people can live safely and authentically.
