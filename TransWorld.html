Worldwide Transgender Statistics

This page highlights key statistics and insights about transgender populations around the world.

Global Population

Approximately 1% of the global population identifies as transgender as stated by the World Population Review done for 2025.

Legal Recognition

Only 96/195 Countries actually recognize Transgender people, Leaving almost 100 countries who oppress and try to get rid of Transgender identities

Health and Social Challenges

Access to healthcare, social acceptance, and protection from discrimination varies widely, making life experiences of transgender individuals highly region-dependent. Even inside of the 96 countries that recognize Transgender people, that doesn't prevent them from being discriminated and attacked by the people living there.
