What Life Is Like for Transgender People in America

These statistics highlight the challenges transgender individuals face across the United States.

Transgender Homelessness

Chapin Hall reports that 1 in 3 transgender adults experience homelessness. Transgender youth face an even higher risk—double that of their non-trans peers.

Transgender Health

According to Chapin Hall, 38% of transgender youth are forced into sexual acts, and 27% resort to trading their bodies to meet basic needs.

Data also shows that 25% have self-harmed and an alarming 62% have been physically attacked.

Transgender Population

The National Library of Medicine reports that approximately 1% of Americans identify as transgender—roughly 560 out of every 100,000 people.
